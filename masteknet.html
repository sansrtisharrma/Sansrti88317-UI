First Steps
My First Salary We understand the value of getting paid on time and compulsorily release salaries on the last day of every month for all Mastekeers, irrespective of the number of days you have been with us. Your salary will be deposited in the company account you opened on the day of your joining.
My Office Timelines

At Mastek, we recognise your potential to be an excellent self-managed professional. Our flextime policy empowers you with the ability to choose your work timings and plan holidays without approvals. You can alter your workday start and finish time as long as it doesn?t interfere with a ?core? period of the day when you are required to be at work. In a week, you will be required to complete 43 hours and 45 min of work (at your own pace).

The total number of leaves allocated for a year is 26. And oh! You also get a complimentary off on your Birthday.
Setup My Workstation

To ease your integration into the Mastek family, we have dedicated teams of in-house staff and IT professionals across all locations who not only assist you in finding the right spot for your workstation but also help you set up the desktop and work to seamlessly resolve all technical issues.
Food Outlets We have some interesting food outlets in and around Mastek office premises. To assist you in navigating these places, we have integrated a map showing a list of such places across locations.

CEO Awards
